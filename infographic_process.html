From Bean to Cup

The Journey of Specialty Coffee ☕

1
Step One
Sourcing
🌱
The journey begins in lush coffee plantations across the equatorial belt. Expert sourcing specialists seek out the finest single-origin beans, evaluating altitude, soil composition, and climate conditions to ensure exceptional quality.
2000+ ORIGINS 70 COUNTRIES
2
Step Two
Roasting
🔥
Raw green beans transform through precise heat application. Master roasters carefully monitor temperature and time to unlock the beans' hidden flavors—creating the perfect balance between acidity, body, and aroma.
150-200°C 12-15 MIN
3
Step Three
Grinding
⚙️
The freshly roasted beans are ground to the optimal particle size for your chosen brewing method. Consistency in grind size determines extraction efficiency and the final cup's clarity and complexity.
WITHIN 15 MIN BURST AROMA
4
Step Four
Brewing
💧
Water meets ground coffee in a carefully choreographed dance. Temperature, timing, and technique combine to extract the desired flavors—whether through pour-over precision, immersion, or espresso pressure.
195-205°F 4:1 RATIO
5
Step Five
Tasting
👃
The moment of truth arrives. Cupping specialists and enthusiasts alike savor the aromatic complexity, assess the acidity and sweetness, and experience the lingering finish that distinguishes exceptional coffee from the ordinary.
100+ FLAVORS NOTES DETECTED
6
Step Six
Enjoying
☕
From farm to your favorite mug, the complete journey culminates in a moment of pure satisfaction. Each sip tells the story of distant mountains, skilled hands, and the art of coffee craftsmanship.
PEAK FLAVOR 60 MINUTES

The Specialty Coffee Experience

Every cup of specialty coffee represents a commitment to quality from sourcing through to your morning ritual. By understanding each step of this journey, you deepen your appreciation for the complexity and care that goes into every brew.
